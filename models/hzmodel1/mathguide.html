Human Zombie Model

This model is a standard $HZ$ model that does not account for any cure (vaccine).

Parameters

$\epsilon$ Rate humans and zombies encounter each other
$t$ Current time
$\beta_i$ Chance zombie bite infects human
$\beta_k$ Chance zombie bite kills human Note: $\beta_k = 1 - \beta_i$
$\beta$ Chance zombie bites human in an encounter
$\kappa$ Chance human kills a zombie in an encounter
$b$ Human birth rate
$N_t$ Total population at time $t$
$S$ Spread, how many zombies cross county borders each time $t$

Equations

Hover over parts of the equation to learn what that part does.

$H_{t+1}$ $=$ $H_t$ $ + bH_t$ $- \frac{\beta \epsilon H_z}{N_t}$ $- S$



$Z_{t+1}$ $=$ $Z_t$ $+ \frac{\epsilon \beta \beta_i H_t Z_t}{N_t}$ $- \frac{\epsilon \kappa H_t Z_t}{N_t}$ $+ S$
